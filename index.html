Help & Support

How to Deposit?

Go to Deposit, enter amount, pay using UPI to the displayed UPI ID, then upload your payment screenshot and enter the UTR number. Your deposit will be verified within 15-30 minutes.

How to Withdraw?

First, set a withdraw password in Settings → Withdraw Password. Then tap Withdraw, select your saved bank account, enter amount and withdraw password. Daily withdrawals are supported!

How do Investment Plans work?

Choose a plan, invest the required amount, and receive daily returns directly to your wallet. You can withdraw your earnings every day!

How does Referral work?

Share your unique referral code with friends. When they sign up using your code and make their first deposit of ₹500 or more, you both earn ₹50 bonus!
